info Description

The Science and Technology Academic and Research-Based Openly-Operated Literacy (STAR) Program aims to support academically gifted Filipino students pursuing specialized STEM strands. This merit-based program provides comprehensive financial support for future scientists, engineers, and researchers committed to national development.

Eligibility Requirements

  • check_circle Incoming 1st year student or graduating Grade 12 student.
  • check_circle GPA of at least 1.25 (or 90% equivalent)
  • check_circle Must belong to the STEM strand (Science, Tech, Engineering, Math).
  • radio_button_unchecked Filipino citizen with no pending immigration application.
